Laurent Park — Araneta City (Megaworld)

Pre-selling & Rent-to-Own Condos • Guided by Dex Mayo

Modern studios, 1BR and 2BR with amenities and flexible payment options in the heart of Cubao.

Laurent Park is Megaworld’s newest residential address inside Araneta City, Cubao. Choose from studio, 1-bedroom, and 2-bedroom layouts (some with balconies) and enjoy an amenity deck with pool, fitness center, function rooms and landscaped open spaces. Live steps from Gateway Mall, Smart Araneta Coliseum and the MRT-3/LRT-2 stations for fast city access. Flexible pre-selling terms and rent-to-own options are available. For price lists, sample computations and current availability, contact Dex Mayo.
